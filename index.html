The capital and largest city in Indonesia, located on the northwest coast of Java. As the center of government, economy, and culture, this metropolis is home to more than 10 million people (with the Greater Jakarta metropolitan area exceeding 23 million). Jakarta is a city of contrasts, combining modern skyscrapers, colonial history, cultural diversity, and rich cuisine.
